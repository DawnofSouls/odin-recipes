Blackguard Big Boggart's Boiled Prawn's Recipe

Boiled Prawn is a dish that is eaten on a regular everyday basis with white rice among the tarnished community.

Feeing defeat? No worries, this will immediately grant you greater strength than never before! Don't be fooled by "boiled" part!

Ingredients

  • ½ pound prawns, peeled and deveined
  • sea salt to taste
  • ½ cup grated coconut
  • 4 Kashmiri chile peppers
  • 1 tablespoon coriander seeds
  • 3 cloves garlic, peeled
  • 5 peppercorns
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 small onion, sliced
  • ¼ teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1 ½ cups water, or as needed
  • 3 ounces okra (bindhi), cut into thirds
  • 3 pieces kokum (fruit from the mangosteen family)
